# Side Quest

Side Quest is a mobile app that gives users a new randomly generated real-world challenge every day.

The goal is to push people out of passive phone consumption and into real-life experiences. Instead of scrolling endlessly, users complete "quests" that encourage exploration, social interaction, and personal growth.

## Example Quests
- Talk to a stranger and learn their name
- Do 50 pushups
- Visit a place in your city you've never been
- Buy a coffee for someone
- Watch the sunrise
- Give someone a genuine compliment
- Try a new restaurant

## Categories
Quests can fall into several categories:
- Social
- Physical
- Exploration
- Personal Growth
- Random / Funny

## Proof System
Users verify completed quests by uploading:
- Photos
- Videos

Completed quests are shared to a social feed inside the app.

## Features (Planned)
- Daily randomized quests
- Photo/video proof uploads
- Social feed
- Friend system
- Leaderboards
- Streak tracking
- Local leagues (city / college / region)

## Vision
Side Quest turns everyday life into a game-like progression system by combining exploration, social interaction, and gamification.
